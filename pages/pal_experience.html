PAL EXPERIENCE

The Philippine Airlines Experience is more than a flight. It’s about making every single journey as special and original as you are, whatever your reason to travel. We’re always here to help you feel relaxed and secure from the airport to boarding and beyond.

Generous baggage allowance
You’ll find generous baggage allowances with free carry-ons, children over 2 are given the same allowance as adults and essentials such as child seats and pushchairs are included.

On-board food and drink
Enjoy great quality Filipino food and drink. We’re passionate about sourcing with integrity and serving the best of the Philippines.

Relax in our lounges
Discover our lounges at airports around the world and experience an extra-special way to travel, with delicious food, plenty of space and attentive service.

An original sense of service

No matter if you're settling in for a movie marathon in our comfortable Economy seats, stretching out in our luxurious flat-bed seats in Business, or relaxing in style with our complimentary pyjamas in First Class, one thing remains constant—our commitment to providing you with the same exceptional Filipino holspitality and service in every cabin.
